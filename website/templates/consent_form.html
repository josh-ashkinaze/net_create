Study Overview

We'll show you 5 common items, and you'll come up with creative uses for each item. To spark your imagination, you'll see ideas from prior participants and even from AI (i.e: ChatGPT). The ideas you submit may be anonymously shown to future participants to spark their imagination. Participation is voluntary and confidential. Ready to get creative?

Benefits & Risks

At the end of the study, we will measure the creativity of each response. The person with the most creative response will get $100. Also, after completing the study we will show you how your creativity compares to humans, to AI, and if you're more creative with or without seeing AI ideas. We don't anticipate any risks of completing this study.
