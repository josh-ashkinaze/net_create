Study Overview

We'll show you 5 items and ask you to come up with a creative use for each item. For some inspiration, we'll also show you ideas from other people who did the experiment. And in some cases, we'll even show you ideas from ChatGPT. The study should will take around 5 minutes. The goal is to learn more about human and human-AI creativity.

Benefits

At the end of the study, we will measure the creativity of each response. The person with the most creative response will get $100. Also, after completing the study we will show you how your creativity compares to humans, to AI, and if you're more creative with or without seeing AI ideas.

More Information

You will be asked to generate creative uses for 5 everyday items. You will be shown responses by prior participants as well as ChatGPT. Your response may be shown to subsequent participants (but no identifying characteristics would be shown -- just the text you submit). Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. All data collected will be kept confidential.

Risks

There are no anticipated risks associated with this study.
