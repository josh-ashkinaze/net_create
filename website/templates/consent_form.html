Study Overview

The purpose of this study is to understand human creativity and how AI (like ChatGPT) affects creativity. We'll show you 5 items and ask you to come up with a creative use for each item. We will show you ideas prior participants had. And in some cases, we will show you ideas that ChatGPT had. The study should take no more than 3 minutes to complete.

Benefits

At the end of the study, we will measure the creativity of each response. The winner will get $100. Also, after completing the study we will show you how your creativity compares to human and ChatGPT.

More Information

You will be asked to generate ideas for an item, and your response may be shown to subsequent participants. Your participation is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time without penalty. All data collected will be kept confidential.

Risks

There are no anticipated risks associated with this study.
